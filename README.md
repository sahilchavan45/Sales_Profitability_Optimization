
# 📊 Sales Profitability Optimization Dashboard

A complete end-to-end Data Analytics project built using **SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Power Query** to identify why business profitability is declining despite strong sales performance.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Businesses often experience high sales but low profitability due to excessive discounts, low-margin products, regional losses, and poor pricing strategies.

This project analyzes **76,524 retail sales records** to uncover the root causes behind declining profit and provides interactive dashboards that support strategic business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The company was experiencing:

- High sales revenue but declining profits
- Heavy discounts reducing overall margins
- Loss-making products and categories
- Regional performance differences
- Seasonal fluctuations impacting profitability

The management required a dashboard that could quickly identify these issues and help improve profitability.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Clean and prepare raw sales data
- Analyze profitability across products, categories, customers, and regions
- Measure the impact of discounts on profit
- Identify loss-making products and regions
- Track seasonal sales and profit trends
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business users

# 📌 Key Business Insights

✔ High discounts significantly reduced profit margins.

✔ Several products generated high sales but consistently produced losses.

✔ Certain regions contributed more revenue but delivered lower profitability.

✔ Seasonal demand impacted both sales and profit performance.

✔ Optimizing pricing and discount strategies can significantly improve profitability.

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive discounting on low-margin products.
- Reprice consistently loss-making products.
- Focus inventory on high-profit categories.
- Improve performance in underperforming regions.
- Monitor seasonal demand for better inventory planning.
- Track profitability using interactive dashboards.

# 📈 Business Impact

- Analyzed **76,524 sales records** to identify profitability drivers.
- Identified loss-making products, regions, and discount patterns affecting business performance.
- Built an executive dashboard enabling faster data-driven decision-making.
- Recommended pricing and discount optimization strategies with an estimated **8% improvement in overall profitability**.
